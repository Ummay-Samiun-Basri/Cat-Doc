Cat Care Tips

Hydration Matters

Encourage your cat to drink fresh water by placing multiple bowls around your home or using a cat fountain.

Regular Grooming

Brush your cat weekly to reduce shedding and hairballs, and to promote healthy skin.

Interactive Play

Engage your cat with toys that stimulate their hunting instincts to keep them active and mentally stimulated.
